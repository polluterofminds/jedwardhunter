J. Edward Hunter

J. Edward Hunter lived most of his life in the Sonoran Desert, and the region still powers his imagination. He currently lives in Dallas with his wife and two sons.

He received his MFA from Arcadia University in 2017. His short stories have appeared in dozens of magazines and journals.

When he isn't writing, you can probably find him on a baseball diamond coaching youth teams or sitting in the stands watching a Major League game.
